Book annual leave

All employees must book annual leave through My Oracle, except teachers and term-time employees. Sign in and select ‘Time and absences’ then ‘Add absence’.

Key information

  • See how much annual leave you have left through My Oracle.
  • Check your total annual leave days here on the intranet
  • You can buy up to 5 more days annual leave every year
  • Your manager will need to approve or decline each annual leave request
  • You must keep your annual leave balance up to date
Read our annual leave policy

Bank holidays

Full-time employees do not need to book bank holidays as annual leave.

Part-time employees who work fewer than 37 hours a week have a bank holiday allowance included in their annual leave entitlement for holiday.

If you work part-time, you should book annual leave on any bank holiday that falls on a day you would usually work.

Check the upcoming bank holidays on GOV.UK

Christmas shutdown day

You must book Christmas shutdown day as annual leave through My Oracle if it falls on a day you would normally work. Christmas shutdown day is already included in your annual leave balance. This year, the Christmas shut down day is 28 December 2022.

Before you book

While you don’t have to, employees often speak to their manager before booking annual leave. It helps them work with you and the team to plan ahead for when you are away from work. Your manager can also book your annual leave for you.
