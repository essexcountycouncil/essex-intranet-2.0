Annual leave allowance

How much annual leave you get is written in your contract. It depends on:

  • conditions of service
  • salary
  • length of service.

Due to the various different types of contracts within the organisation, you can check your annual leave entitlement in our Holiday Policy (PDF, 165KB) or check your contract.

Find out how much annual leave you currently have

You can check how much holiday you have on My Oracle. Go to My Oracle, Time and Absences, Absence Balance.

Your absence balance is shown in hours and will include:

  • any holiday hours that you have bought
  • bank holiday hours if you work part-time
  • a deduction for any approved annual leave

Buying annual leave cost calculator

Use this calculator to work out the total cost and cost per day of buying more annual leave.

Recording extra annual leave

You should record your extra annual leave locally. As it’s not part of your standard annual leave entitlement, it won’t show in your entitlement balance in My Oracle. ​
